Laptop Evaluation Policy

How we assess, evaluate, and price pre-owned or non-functional laptops.

Evaluation Process

01
Physical Assessment

We inspect the exterior for cracks, hinge stability, screen scratches/dents, and keyboard condition.

02
Hardware Diagnostics

Testing the RAM, Storage health (SSD/HDD), Battery cycles, and Processor performance under load.

03
Functional Audit

Verification of all ports (USB, HDMI, Charging), Wi-Fi/Bluetooth connectivity, Webcam, and Speakers.

04
Dead Laptop Assessment

For units that do not power on, we evaluate the value of individual salvageable components such as the LCD panel, keyboard, palmrest, and internal casing.

Minimum Generation Requirement

To maintain our quality standards, Nexcom Ventures only purchases or accepts laptops powered by Intel 4th Generation processors or newer (e.g., i3/i5/i7-4xxx and above). We do not purchase older "Legacy" models (Core 2 Duo, Intel 1st/2nd/3rd Gen).

How We Price

Our valuation is derived from the current market value of functional parts minus the estimated labor required for refurbishment or dismantling. We strive to provide the most competitive and fair valuation in the market for your hardware.
