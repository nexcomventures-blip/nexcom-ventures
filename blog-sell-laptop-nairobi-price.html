Factors That Affect Your Laptop's Price

1. RAM and Storage: 16GB RAM and SSD storage commands significantly more than 4GB RAM with an HDD. Upgrading RAM before selling can increase your offer.

2. Battery health: A laptop with 80%+ battery health is worth considerably more. A swollen or dead battery drops the price.

3. Screen condition: Cracks, dead pixels, or severe scratches reduce the value. A working, clean screen is a big plus.

4. Original receipt and box: Not essential, but can boost your offer — especially for MacBooks.

5. Age and generation: An 8th-gen Intel vs a 10th-gen Intel makes a difference. Newer chips = higher price.

How to Get the Best Price for Your Laptop in Nairobi

Before bringing your laptop in: clean it properly, charge it to full, gather the charger and any accessories. If you have the original box, bring it. Delete your personal data (factory reset if possible). A laptop that looks well cared for always fetches more.
